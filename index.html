FAQ

Common questions.

How fast can I get my ads? +

Starter packages deliver in 7 days, Professional in 10, and Premium (with ad management) in 14 days from brief approval.

Do you run the ads or just design them? +

Design-only for Starter. Professional includes campaign setup. Premium includes full 7-day ad management with optimization and a report.

What do you need from me to get started? +

Your product photos or raw footage, brand colors/logo if you have them, and a short brief on your target customer and offer.

Can I request revisions? +

Yes — every package includes revisions, from 2 rounds on Starter up to unlimited minor revisions on Premium.

Do you work with brands outside India? +

Yes, FX Studio works with clients globally. Pricing shown is in INR but I quote in USD/other currencies on request.
